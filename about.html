About Me

My name is Raul Marquez III and I'm a first-year master's student majoring in mathematics at the University of Rio Grande Valley. Currently, I'm in the 4+1 program for the mathematics department, so I may receive a Bachelors and Masters of Science in a four-year program. I've attended an REU at William's College SMALL Program and the the UT Austin DRP Summer Program. Currently, I'm working as a research assistant on extremal set problems with Professor Alexey Glazyrin.
